Forever

Forever and always by your side,

I'll walk beside you every stride,

a quest i hope you do not mind,

a quest with which together we'll find,

completion in the life we live,

and you'll have everything I can give.


people come and go they say,

but i don't really think that way,

i care for few and love few less,

it's just who i am i guess.


but one thing i hold so close to heart,

something i've valued from the start:

my family, by blood and choice,

those who recognise the quivers in my voice,

those who see through the mask i hold,

those who bring me warmth in cold.


of that, you, my family,

this is our reality,

despite potential brutality,

or a sense of inhumanity,

misunderstood triviality,

underestimated calamity,

beginning to end and back again,

you'll always have that one small friend,

waiting at the threshold of that one small home,

pur home in the middle of the world we roam.


forever and always at that door,

forever and always, forever and more,

you've looked back to see them glare up at you,

but you looked to the side, no one in view,

today look beside you and you will see,

that one small friend, that which is me.
