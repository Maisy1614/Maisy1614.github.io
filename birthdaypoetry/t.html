22

Twenty-two years in this world done,

twenty-two years of struggle and fun,

twenty-two years on the pursuit of knowledge,

twenty-two years i must acknowledge,

kinda makes you a boomer in my eyes,

but i digress, no time for lies.


the value of time, a joke before,

a joke before you calmed the war,

the internal war that raged on forth,

reducing human to a swarth,

one i couldn't calm alone,

'cause one of us had to claim the throne.


like vengeful wolves of rival packs,

waiting for foe to turn their backs,

digging their claws into their enemy's hinds,

escaping to the left of their right minds,

dejected at loss they walk away,

but slowly their own thoughts begin to prey,

on the very side they'd fought for,

their own thoughts they so abhor.


so the question posed, one of its kind,

are the enemies on the border or is the enemy inside?

a twisting case of vrais and faux,

an answer leaving you in awe,

but in the end it did not matter,

for rescue came along to shatter,

the resentment hid behind the battle.


rescue came along to rattle,

the heart and mind of the growling wolf,

a feeling of safety threatening to engulf,

the pack in whole, not just a few,

that rescue in the form of you.
