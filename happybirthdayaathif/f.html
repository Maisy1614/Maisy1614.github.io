Forever

Forever and always by your side,

I'll walk beside you every stride,

a request i hope you do not mind,

a request with which together we'll find,

completion in the life we live,

you'll have everything I can give.


people come and go they say,

but i don't think i considered that anyway,

i care for few and love few less,

it's just who i am i guess.


but one thing i hold so close to my heart,

that nothing will tear my love for apart,

is my family, by blood and choice,

those who recognise the quivers in my voice,

the ones who see through the mask i hold,

the ones who bring me warmth in cold.


of that, you, my family,

this is our reality,

despite potential brutality,

or senses of inhumanity,

misunderstood triviality,

underestimated calamity,

beginning to end and back again,

you'll always have one small friend,

waiting at the threshold of one small home,

in the middle of a bigger world we roam.


forever and always at that door,

forever and always, forever and more,

once you looked behind as they looked up at you,

but you looked to the side to see your shadow,

today look beside you and you will see,

something less black, that which is me.
