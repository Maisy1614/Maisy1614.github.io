Perfect

A loud thud does sound,

a little stiff body on the ground,

a cry for help amidst the night,

a caterwaul: "let's make this right"


alas they're only silent screams,

for help does only come in dreams,

but is that not what makes this right?

shining armour with no knight...


you see yourself but a normal man,

maybe one with a cracked up hand,

maybe on the outside looking in,

with a thousand stories marked on skin.


but i see you better, i promise you,

for you are one to see me through,

the only one with eyes so true,

the one who came to my rescue,

for i've sunk and sunk but never drowned,

but still no breath of air i found,

my lungs deflate for one last time,

the hour of death does play its chime,

and I let go of all I know,

but life you never let me throw,

I fell to the seabed, not long left,

but you held on, a legal theft,

a silent saviour, is that not it?

is that not you, does the shoe not fit?


I see you better, I promise you,

those piercing eyes I watch them chew,

right past the walls I built so high,

straight to my lungs to drain them dry,

awake I come, still in your arms,

away you spin me in your charms,

a hundred deaths passed but one-o-one,

is a different journey I've begun.


the perfect laugh starts to shake,

the joys within me right awake,

for only one sound will wake me up,

like a lightning bolt in me struck,

the sound of your voice, soft and tender,

who knew you'd be myself's first mender?


but beyond that is what you are,

you are in my sky the shining star,

never to die, never goodbye,

never to be seen in another sky,

a wet seed in the soil of life I was,

never allowed to grow because,

of all the loves that have passed me by,

some too strong, some too dry,

a raging storm within my sky,

could never let me grow too high,

a careful drizzle rains on me,

never enough to set me free,

so what did i need? unknown to me.


until you rowed in on your bright grey clouds,

my little sky now in a shroud,

the perfect storm, not fast not slow,

the perfect storm for me to grow,

but somehow the sun still shines through,

no surprise, the sun is you,

the shining star in my sky,

the shining star for all of time.


hold my hand and we'll brave this together,

hand in hand, together forever,

a story to hear and a story to tell,

infinite stories until farewell.


the perfect grey is now in sight,

because nothing right is black or white,

another lesson yet to learn,

another joy yet to earn,

but i see you now, is that not right?

does the shoe not fit, a little tight?

that's okay, a lesson learned,

nothing is perfect, all is flawed,

all but you, for you have earned,

perfect, perfect, to be called.
